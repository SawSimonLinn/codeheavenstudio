# Firebase Studio

This is a NextJS starter in Firebase Studio.

To get started, take a look at src/app/page.tsx.

## Legal Policy Dates

The effective and last-updated dates for legal pages are centralized in `src/lib/legal-metadata.ts`.
When Terms or Privacy content changes, update `EFFECTIVE_DATE` and/or `LAST_UPDATED_DATE` in that file so both pages stay in sync.
